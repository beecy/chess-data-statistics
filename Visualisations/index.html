White vs Black

On average White wins 1.55 times more than Black - most likely because it can always "dictate" the opening.
But it really comes down to how much you need opening theory to win chess games at your level. The lower you are rated, the less you really need to depend on openings to win and consequently, the choice of colors might not really matter.

Control The Centre

Controlling the centre means:
1. Greater influence over the board.
2. Greater mobility of pieces.

Carefully Develop Your Pieces

1. Don't bring the queen out early.
2. Develop minor pieces first - usually knights before bishops.

White vs Black

In chess, the player who makes the first move has a slight advantage - which happens to be white.

Strategically position your pieces

Positioning your pieces at certain key places can drastically improve their potential.

Journey Of A Gallant Knight
Path taken by g1 Knight throughout his life

A knight has the ability to jump over other pieces which makes it a powerful asset and studying the path taken by it can be very beneficial.

The above visualisations and analysis is based on over 250k games of chess played between GrandMaster level players at renowned competitions. These games were collected in PGN format from websites including FIDE - World Chess Federation and Chess.com.
